As healthcare technology continues to advance at an unprecedented pace, the importance of protecting sensitive patient data has never been more pressing. The rapid adoption of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) in healthcare has created new challenges for ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of patient information.

The Problem

The sheer volume of patient data generated by electronic health records (EHRs), medical imaging, and wearable devices poses significant risks to data security. With more clinicians accessing and sharing patient data electronically, the potential for data breaches and unauthorized disclosure increases exponentially. Furthermore, the use of AI-powered tools in healthcare has introduced new vulnerabilities, such as biased algorithms and insufficient data validation.
