The Problem: Real Challenges in Edge Computing

Current edge computing solutions face significant challenges:

1. Power Consumption: Most existing solutions rely on resource-intensive processing, leading to high power consumption and heat generation. 2. Data Transfer Latency: Transferring large amounts of data between devices and cloud-based services introduces unacceptable latency, compromising real-time decision-making capabilities. 3. Scalability: As the number of edge devices grows, maintaining scalability becomes increasingly difficult.

Technical Deep Dive: Architecture, Algorithms, Implementation

Clisonix addresses these challenges through a novel approach to power-efficient AI at the edge. Our solution leverages cutting-edge technologies like the Tide Engine and Signal Fabric:

1. Tide Engine: This innovative technology ensures consistent state across distributed healthcare nodes by employing advanced synchronization algorithms. 2. Signal Fabric: By weaving together EEG, audio, and biosensor streams, Signal Fabric provides a unified framework for processing diverse data modalities.

Our architecture is designed to optimize power efficiency through:

1. In-Situ Processing: Real-time processing occurs on the device itself, eliminating unnecessary data transfer. 2. Model Pruning: Our system employs model pruning techniques to minimize memory usage and power consumption.
