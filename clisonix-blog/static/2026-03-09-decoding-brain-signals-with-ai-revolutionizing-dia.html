Technical Deep Dive

Our team has developed an AI-driven system that utilizes machine learning algorithms to analyze brain signals in real-time. This is made possible by Clisonix's CRDT Merge Layer, which ensures seamless collaboration among multiple clinicians working on the same patient data simultaneously. The layer enables near-instant updates and avoids version control conflicts.

To secure sensitive medical data, we employ Post-Quantum Encryption, a future-proof solution that protects against potential quantum computer threats. This encryption method not only safeguards patient information but also facilitates compliant data sharing between institutions.

The Tide Engine plays a crucial role in maintaining the system's consistency across distributed healthcare nodes. By ensuring each node is aware of its surroundings and adapts to changes in real-time, our system can efficiently process and analyze vast amounts of brain signal data.
