Technical Deep Dive

To grasp the full implications of the EU's AI Act, let's dive into the technical details. The legislation introduces several key concepts:

1. Explainability: AI systems must provide clear and transparent explanations for their decisions. 2. Accountability: Manufacturers are responsible for ensuring that their AI systems operate in accordance with EU law. 3. Human Oversight: Humans must be able to intervene and correct any errors or biases introduced by the AI system.
