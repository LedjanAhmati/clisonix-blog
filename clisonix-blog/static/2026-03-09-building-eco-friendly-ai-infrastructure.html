Technical Deep Dive: Architecture, Algorithms, Implementation

Our journey begins with the Tide Engine (TE), a distributed computing framework designed for healthcare applications. TE ensures consistent state across nodes while maintaining high throughput, even in the presence of partial failures. This is critical for large-scale AI deployments where data is constantly being ingested and processed.

Next, we have Signal Fabric (SF), an innovative streaming platform that integrates EEG, audio, and biosensor streams into a cohesive signal processing framework. SF's modular design allows us to seamlessly incorporate new sensor types and algorithms as research evolves. This adaptability is essential for addressing the diverse needs of healthcare AI applications.
