The Problem

Developing and deploying AI/ML models is a complex process that involves multiple stakeholders, infrastructure, and workflows. However, when it comes to scaling up these systems, several challenges arise:

1. Data processing latency: As data volumes grow, the time it takes for ML models to process and make decisions becomes unacceptable. 2. Infrastructure costs: Maintaining high-performance computing infrastructure is expensive, especially in edge settings where power and cooling are limited. 3. Model drift and bias: As data distributions change over time, ML models can become inaccurate or biased, compromising their effectiveness.
