The Problem: Real Challenges in Regulatory Compliance

Regulatory compliance is a minefield of complexities, and the stakes are high. Non-compliance can result in severe financial penalties, reputational damage, and even loss of licensure. Healthcare organizations must grapple with multiple regulatory frameworks, including HIPAA, ICD-10, and FDA guidelines for medical device software. The sheer volume of data generated by electronic health records (EHRs), medical devices, and AI-driven systems makes it increasingly difficult to maintain accurate audit trails.

Moreover, the rise of decentralized healthcare networks, IoT devices, and edge computing introduces new challenges in ensuring data integrity and provenance. As a result, healthcare organizations are turning to innovative solutions that leverage advanced technologies like artificial intelligence (AI) and blockchain to build robust audit trails.
