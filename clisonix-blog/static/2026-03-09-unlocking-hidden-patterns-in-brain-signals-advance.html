Signal processing remains a significant challenge in neuroscience research. Existing methods often rely on manual analysis or simplistic algorithms, which fail to capture the nuances and intricacies of brain activity. Technical Deep Dive

At Clisonix, we employ advanced techniques to address these challenges. LIAM is a binary algebra-based system that enables real-time analysis and pattern recognition. By leveraging matrix algebra and tensor operations, LIAM extracts complex relationships between signal data points, providing unparalleled insight into brain function. Our implementation includes:
