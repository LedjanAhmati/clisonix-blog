The Problem

Current AI systems in healthcare struggle to provide actionable insights from EEG data due to several challenges:

1. Scalability: Processing large datasets of EEG signals while maintaining accuracy is a significant hurdle. 2. Interpretation: Identifying meaningful patterns in neural activity remains an open problem.
