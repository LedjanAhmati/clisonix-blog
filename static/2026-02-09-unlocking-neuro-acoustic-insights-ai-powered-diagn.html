The Problem: Real Challenges in AI-ML Systems

While the prospect of AI-driven diagnostics is promising, several challenges have hindered its widespread adoption:

* Scalability and latency: Traditional AI models require significant computational resources, leading to high latency and limited edge-to-cloud inference capabilities. * Signal transformations: Complex signal processing tasks often rely on Python loops, hindering performance and interpretability. * Task scheduling: Deterministic task scheduling is critical for real-time applications, but existing solutions can introduce complexity and errors.
