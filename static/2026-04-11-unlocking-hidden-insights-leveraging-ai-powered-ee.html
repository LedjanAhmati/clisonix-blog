The Problem: Challenges in Signal Processing

Traditional signal processing methods often fall short when dealing with complex EEG data. The sheer volume of information, the variability of brain activity patterns, and the need for real-time analysis make it a daunting task for clinicians and researchers alike. Moreover, existing solutions frequently rely on manual annotation or time-consuming, labor-intensive processes that hinder scalability.
