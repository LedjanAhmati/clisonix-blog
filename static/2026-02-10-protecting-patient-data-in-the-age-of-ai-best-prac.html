The Problem: Real Challenges in Data Privacy

Healthcare organizations face numerous challenges when it comes to safeguarding patient data:

* Rising complexity: The increasing use of AI-powered tools has led to an explosion of sensitive health information, which must be securely stored and processed. * Regulatory compliance: Healthcare providers must adhere to increasingly stringent regulations, such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). * Cybersecurity threats: The expanding attack surface created by AI-powered systems has attracted a new breed of sophisticated cyber threats.
