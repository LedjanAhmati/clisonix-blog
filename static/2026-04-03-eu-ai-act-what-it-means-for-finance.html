In April 2021, the European Union unveiled its Artificial Intelligence Act (EU AI Act), which aims to regulate the development and use of AI systems within the EU. The proposed regulation has significant implications for organizations in the finance sector, who must adapt their strategies to comply with the new standards.

Financial institutions are among the most heavily regulated sectors, with a complex web of rules and regulations governing their operations. The proposed regulation requires organizations to ensure that their AI systems meet standards related to transparency, accountability, and bias prevention.
