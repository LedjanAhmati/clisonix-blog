The Problem: Real Challenges in Audio Processing

Audio processing in healthcare involves dealing with large datasets, multiple modalities (e.g., EEG, ECG), and high-frequency signals. Traditional methods often struggle to handle these complexities, resulting in decreased accuracy, increased latency, and higher computational costs. Furthermore, the lack of standardized protocols and data formats exacerbates the issue, making it difficult for clinicians and researchers to share findings across institutions.

Technical Deep Dive: Architecture, Algorithms, Implementation
