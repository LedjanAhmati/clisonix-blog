The healthcare industry is on the cusp of a revolution, driven by the rapid adoption of Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies. However, this progress is threatened by the increasing concerns around data privacy and security. With the rise of IoT devices, wearable sensors, and EEG analytics, healthcare providers are generating vast amounts of sensitive patient data. It's imperative to develop transparent AI systems that safeguard this information while ensuring accurate diagnoses and effective treatments.

The Problem

The current state of data privacy in healthcare is precarious. A 2020 report by the World Health Organization (WHO) highlighted that over 90% of healthcare organizations experience some form of data breach, compromising sensitive patient information. Moreover, a survey by Accenture found that nearly half of patients are hesitant to share their health data due to concerns about its security and misuse.

To address these challenges, we need AI systems that can ensure the integrity and confidentiality of patient data while providing actionable insights for healthcare professionals. Clisonix's Tide Engine and Signal Fabric technologies are designed to tackle this problem by creating a secure, distributed framework for EEG analytics.
