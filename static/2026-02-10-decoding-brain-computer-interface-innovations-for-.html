The Problem: Real Challenges in Sustainable Tech

Healthcare technology has traditionally relied on resource-intensive systems that generate significant amounts of electronic waste. The healthcare sector accounts for approximately 4% of global greenhouse gas emissions, with a substantial portion attributed to data centers and medical imaging equipment (1). Moreover, the reliance on non-renewable energy sources and the lack of efficient cooling systems exacerbate these issues.
