The Problem

Parkinson's disease is a neurodegenerative disorder that affects millions of people worldwide. Early diagnosis is crucial for effective management, but current methods rely heavily on clinical assessment, which can be subjective and time-consuming. There is an urgent need for objective and reliable diagnostic tools to aid in the early detection of Parkinson's.

Technical Deep Dive

Our approach involves collecting audio samples from patients with Parkinson's disease and healthy controls, using these samples to train a deep neural network (DNN) model.
