The Problem: Challenges in Sustainable Tech

Sustainable technology must not only reduce environmental impact but also prioritize human health and well-being. In healthcare, this means developing solutions that minimize energy consumption, reduce e-waste, and promote eco-friendly practices. However, the industry faces significant challenges:

* High energy consumption due to data-intensive applications * Increasing electronic waste generated by outdated medical devices * Limited scalability of existing solutions

To overcome these hurdles, we need innovative approaches that integrate sustainable technologies with healthcare innovations.

Technical Deep Dive: Architecture and Algorithms

At Clisonix, our Signal Fabric platform weaves together EEG, audio, and biosensor streams to create a comprehensive picture of patient data. This architecture ensures seamless integration with other technologies like Tide Engine, which ensures consistent state across distributed healthcare nodes. Our proprietary algorithms analyze brain wave patterns, allowing us to detect subtle changes in cognitive function.

Our approach is built on the following key components:

* EEG Data Preprocessing: Utilizing our Signal Fabric platform, we preprocess EEG data by filtering out noise and artifacts, allowing for accurate analysis of brain wave patterns. * Feature Extraction: Our algorithms extract relevant features from EEG signals, such as spectral power, coherence, and phase-locking value (PLV). * Classification and Pattern Recognition: We utilize machine learning techniques to classify patients based on their brain wave patterns, enabling early detection of cognitive impairments.
