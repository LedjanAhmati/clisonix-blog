The Problem: Real Challenges in Data Privacy

In fact, a recent study found that nearly 70% of healthcare organizations have experienced a data breach in the past two years alone. Furthermore, as we increasingly rely on cloud-based services to store and process sensitive medical information, the threat landscape has grown more complex.
