The Problem: Real Challenges in AI-ML Systems

While AI and ML hold tremendous promise for healthcare, several challenges hinder their widespread adoption. These include:

1. Data complexity: EEG signals are inherently noisy and complex, making it difficult to extract meaningful insights. 2. Interpretability: The lack of transparency in AI decision-making processes can make it challenging for healthcare professionals to trust the recommendations.
