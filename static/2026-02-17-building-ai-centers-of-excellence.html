The Problem

Despite the growing demand for AI-driven solutions in healthcare, many organizations struggle to establish effective AI CoEs. Common challenges include:

1. Scalability: As datasets grow, traditional architectures often falter under the weight of increased computational demands. 2. To overcome these challenges, it's essential to develop AI systems that are scalable, consistent, and low-latent.
