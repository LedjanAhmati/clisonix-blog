The Problem: Real Challenges in AI-ML Systems

AI-ML systems are inherently complex due to their data-driven nature, multiple stakeholders, and continuous learning processes. As a result, identifying issues in real-time is essential for maintaining system reliability and performance. However, this complexity also introduces several challenges:

* Monitoring Data Quality: With the sheer volume of data generated by AI-ML systems, ensuring data quality is crucial for accurate model predictions. * Model Drift Detection: As models are continuously updated or fine-tuned, it's essential to detect drifts in performance and accuracy. * Resource Utilization: Managing compute resources, memory allocation, and network bandwidth is critical to prevent system crashes and optimize performance.
