The Problem: Energy Consumption in Medical Devices

Medical devices, such as electroencephalography (EEG) machines, require significant energy to operate. These devices often consume a substantial amount of power, which contributes to greenhouse gas emissions and increases healthcare costs. As the global demand for healthcare services continues to rise, it's crucial to develop sustainable solutions that prioritize both patient care and environmental responsibility.
