The Problem: Real Challenges in Edge Computing

Edge computing presents a unique set of hurdles for healthcare applications like BCIs. The sheer volume of data generated by EEG, audio, and biosensors requires sophisticated processing capabilities that are not always feasible at the edge. Moreover, real-time analysis demands ultra-low latency and predictability, making it difficult to ensure consistent performance across distributed nodes.
