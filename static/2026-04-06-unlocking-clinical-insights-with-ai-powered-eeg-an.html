The Problem: Real Challenges in AI ML Systems

Despite the tremendous potential of AI and ML to transform healthcare, their adoption is often hindered by several challenges. These include:

* Data quality and availability: EEG data is notoriously noisy and variable, requiring sophisticated preprocessing and analysis techniques. * Computational complexity: Large-scale EEG datasets demand significant computational resources, which can be a bottleneck in real-world clinical settings. * Interpretability and explainability: As AI models become increasingly complex, their ability to provide actionable insights into brain function becomes more opaque.
