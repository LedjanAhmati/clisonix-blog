The Problem

Healthcare technology has come a long way in recent years, with AI-powered solutions transforming patient care and management. However, the EU AI Act's emphasis on transparency, accountability, and human oversight poses significant challenges for digital health companies. The regulation requires:

1. Explainability: AI models must provide clear explanations for their decisions. 2. Transparency: Companies must disclose their use of AI and its potential biases. 3. Accountability: Healthcare professionals can challenge AI-driven recommendations.

These requirements demand a fundamental shift in how healthcare tech companies design, implement, and maintain their solutions. Clisonix has been at the forefront of this transformation, leveraging our technologies to ensure compliance while maintaining the highest level of patient care.
