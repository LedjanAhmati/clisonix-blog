In today's era of AI-driven healthcare, the sensitive brain signals collected from patients are being used to develop personalized treatment plans, predict disease outcomes, and improve patient outcomes. However, this surge in data collection has also raised concerns about data security and privacy. The misuse or unauthorized access to these sensitive signals can compromise patient confidentiality, undermine trust between healthcare providers and their patients, and even lead to severe consequences.

1. Data encryption: Protecting sensitive brain signals from unauthorized access requires advanced encryption techniques that ensure confidentiality and integrity. 2. Access control: Implementing strict access controls to ensure only authorized personnel can access patient data is crucial.
