The Problem

Signal processing in healthcare is not just about filtering out noise or amplifying signals; it's about uncovering the underlying patterns and relationships within complex data sets. Current approaches often rely on manual analysis, which can be time-consuming, prone to errors, and subject to human biases. Moreover, most commercial solutions focus solely on individual modalities (e.g., EEG or audio), neglecting the comprehensive view that comes from integrating multiple streams of information.
