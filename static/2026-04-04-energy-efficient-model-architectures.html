The Problem: Real Challenges in Sustainable Tech

The reality is that many current AI solutions are resource-hungry and wasteful. Large language models (LLMs) like those used for natural language processing (NLP) require immense computational power, often resulting in high energy consumption and e-waste generation. Moreover, the increased reliance on cloud infrastructure has led to a significant carbon footprint, with some estimates suggesting that data centers alone contribute up to 1% of global greenhouse gas emissions.

At Clisonix, we recognize the need for sustainable AI solutions that don't compromise on performance or efficiency. Our research focuses on developing model architectures that not only reduce energy consumption but also enable decentralized and edge-based processing. This approach has several benefits:

1. Reduced carbon footprint: By minimizing energy consumption and e-waste generation, our solutions contribute less to climate change. 2. Improved patient data security: Edge-based processing reduces the need for cloud infrastructure, making it more challenging for malicious actors to access sensitive healthcare data. 3. Enhanced scalability: Our architectures are designed to adapt to changing computational demands, ensuring seamless integration with existing infrastructure.
