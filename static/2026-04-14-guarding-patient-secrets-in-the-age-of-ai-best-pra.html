The Problem: Real Challenges in Data Privacy

As healthcare providers transition towards more sophisticated AI-powered diagnostic tools, they must confront the daunting task of ensuring patient data security. A single data breach can have far-reaching consequences, not only compromising sensitive information but also eroding trust between patients and healthcare professionals. The consequences are dire:

* Regulatory Non-Compliance: Failure to adhere to HIPAA guidelines can result in hefty fines and reputational damage. * Patient Trust Erosion: Data breaches can lead to a decline in patient satisfaction, loyalty, and ultimately, the quality of care received. * Intellectual Property Theft: Unsecured EEG data can be exploited for malicious purposes, compromising research findings and potentially leading to life-threatening consequences.
