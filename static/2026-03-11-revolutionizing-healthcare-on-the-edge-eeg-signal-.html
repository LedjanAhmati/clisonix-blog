The Problem: Challenges in Edge Computing

Edge computing's primary advantage lies in its ability to process data closer to where it is generated. However, this proximity also presents unique challenges when it comes to healthcare applications, particularly those involving EEG signals. These signals are sensitive and require high accuracy for reliable analysis.

To address these challenges, we need to consider two key aspects: processing latency and computational complexity. EEG signal processing often involves complex algorithms that demand significant computational resources. When done on-premises or in the cloud, this can lead to unacceptable delays between data acquisition and analysis.

Moreover, healthcare institutions typically operate with distributed infrastructure, comprising multiple nodes across various locations. Ensuring consistent state management across these nodes is essential for real-time data processing. This is where Tide Engine comes into play – our proprietary technology that guarantees a reliable and consistent state across all distributed nodes in the Clisonix network.
