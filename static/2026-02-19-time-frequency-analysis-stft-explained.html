One major issue is the difficulty in extracting meaningful features from complex signals. As we all know, Fourier Transform (FT) is a powerful tool for analyzing frequency content within signals. However, its limitations become apparent when dealing with non-stationary signals ‚Äì those that exhibit time-varying spectral properties.

Here's where STFT comes into play: by applying the windowed FT approach to segments of the signal, we can effectively decompose complex signals into their constituent components, revealing hidden patterns and relationships.

Technical Deep Dive: Architecture, Algorithms, Implementation
