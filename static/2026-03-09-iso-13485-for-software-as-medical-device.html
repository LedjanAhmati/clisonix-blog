The Problem

Regulatory agencies like the FDA and CE mark have established stringent guidelines for SaMD to ensure that they meet medical device regulations. However, integrating AI-powered software into existing healthcare systems poses significant technical and compliance hurdles:

1. Data Security: Protecting sensitive patient data from unauthorized access or breaches. 2. Clinical Validation: Demonstrating the safety and efficacy of AI-driven diagnoses or treatments. 3. Software Development: Ensuring consistent state management across distributed nodes, integrating multiple streams (e.g., EEG, audio, biosensors), and implementing scalable architecture.
