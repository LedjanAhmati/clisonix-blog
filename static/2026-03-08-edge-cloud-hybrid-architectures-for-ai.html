The Problem: Challenges in Edge Computing

Edge computing is not without its challenges. Traditional architectures often struggle to handle the vast amounts of data generated by medical devices and wearables, while cloud-based solutions can introduce latency, security risks, and high operational costs. Moreover, many edge computing applications are limited by their inability to adapt to changing network conditions or handle varying workloads.

To overcome these limitations, Clisonix has developed its own edge-cloud hybrid architecture, which combines the strengths of both paradigms. Our approach ensures seamless communication between edge devices and cloud-based services, enabling real-time data processing, efficient resource utilization, and enhanced security features.
