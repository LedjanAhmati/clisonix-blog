Technical Deep Dive: Architecture, Algorithms, Implementation

Our solution leverages two key Clisonix technologies:

1. Tide Engine: Our proprietary engine ensures consistent state across distributed healthcare nodes, guaranteeing seamless data synchronization and minimizing latency. 2. Signal Fabric: This innovative module weaves together EEG, audio, and biosensor streams to create a unified, high-fidelity dataset for advanced analysis.

Here's an overview of our architecture:

* EEG signals are collected from patients using wearable headsets or scalp-mounted sensors. * The data is transmitted wirelessly to the Signal Fabric module, which processes and integrates it with other relevant sensor readings (e.g., audio, biosignals). * The processed signal streams are then fed into the Tide Engine for distributed processing and real-time analytics.

Our algorithmic framework incorporates machine learning techniques to identify patterns in brain activity, enabling early diagnosis, treatment optimization, and personalized care planning.
