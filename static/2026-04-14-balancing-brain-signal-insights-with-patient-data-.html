The Problem: Real Challenges in Data Privacy

As healthcare providers continue to integrate AI into their workflows, they are faced with a daunting task: balancing the benefits of AI-driven insights with the need for robust patient data protection. The consequences of neglecting data privacy can be severe, including compromised patient confidentiality, regulatory fines, and even liability lawsuits.

According to a recent survey by the Health Information Trust Alliance (HITRUST), 75% of healthcare organizations reported experiencing at least one security incident in the past year. These incidents can lead to significant financial losses, damage to reputation, and erosion of trust among patients.
