Technical Deep Dive

At the core of our solution lies Tide Engine, a distributed state management system that ensures consistent state across multiple nodes. This enables real-time data processing while maintaining high levels of availability and scalability. Building on top of Tide Engine is Signal Fabric, which weaves together EEG, audio, and biosensor streams to create a unified view of patient data.

Our edge-optimized AI architecture leverages the power of binary algebra to analyze EEG signals in real-time. This allows for the detection of subtle patterns and anomalies that may indicate neurological disorders or other health issues.
