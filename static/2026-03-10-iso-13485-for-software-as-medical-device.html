FAQ

❓ What is ISO 13485, and why is it relevant to software as a medical device?

ISO 13485 is an international standard for medical devices that requires companies to demonstrate compliance with rigorous quality management systems. As healthcare AI becomes increasingly prevalent, ensuring compliance with this standard is crucial.

❓ How does Tide Engine ensure consistent state across distributed healthcare nodes?

Tide Engine uses a combination of distributed locking mechanisms and consensus protocols to maintain a consistent global state across nodes, ensuring data integrity and consistency.

❓ What are the benefits of using Signal Fabric in our solution?

Signal Fabric enables real-time data streaming and processing, allowing us to identify patterns and correlations that would be difficult or impossible to detect with traditional methods.

❓ Can you provide more information on the LIAM Binary Algebra?

The LIAM Binary Algebra is a novel approach to matrix algebra operations, enabling us to perform complex calculations in real-time. This allows for faster processing of large datasets and improved decision-making.
