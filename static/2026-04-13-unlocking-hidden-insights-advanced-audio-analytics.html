As the healthcare industry continues to grapple with the complexities of patient care, there has never been a greater need for accurate and timely clinical decision support systems (CDSSs). One often-overlooked aspect of CDSSs is audio analytics – the process of extracting meaningful insights from various types of audio data. With the increasing availability of wearable devices, smart sensors, and mobile health apps, the amount of audio data being generated in healthcare settings has grown exponentially.

The Problem

Real-time audio processing for CDSSs poses several challenges:

1. Signal complexity: Audio signals are inherently complex, with multiple frequency components and varying amplitudes. 2. Noise and artifact removal: Real-world audio data often contains noise and artifacts that can mask or distort the underlying signal of interest. 3. Data synchronization: Multiple sources of audio data may need to be synchronized for accurate analysis, which can be challenging in real-time settings.

Technical Deep Dive

At Clisonix, we've developed a comprehensive solution to these challenges using our Signal Fabric and Tide Engine technologies. Signal Fabric is an advanced signal processing framework that seamlessly integrates EEG, audio, and biosensor streams into a unified data structure. This enables us to apply sophisticated algorithms for noise reduction, artifact removal, and signal feature extraction.
