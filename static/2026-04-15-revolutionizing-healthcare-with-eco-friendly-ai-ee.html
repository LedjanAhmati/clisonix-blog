The Problem

Traditional healthcare systems face numerous challenges when it comes to incorporating sustainable practices. The sheer volume of data generated from various sources – medical images, patient records, and sensor readings – poses significant hurdles in terms of storage, processing, and analysis. Moreover, the increasing demand for real-time insights and decision-making support puts immense pressure on existing infrastructure.

Current diagnostic methods often rely on invasive procedures or expensive equipment, contributing to healthcare's ecological footprint. Furthermore, the lack of standardization and interoperability across different systems hinders seamless data exchange and integration, exacerbating these problems.

Technical Deep Dive

Clisonix has pioneered an innovative solution by harnessing the power of EEG (electroencephalography) and audio analytics in a sustainable diagnostic framework.
