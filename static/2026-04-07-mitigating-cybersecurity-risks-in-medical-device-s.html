Clisonix is at the forefront of this innovation, and our Tide Engine ensures consistent state across distributed healthcare nodes, while Signal Fabric weaves together EEG, audio, and biosensor streams.

The Problem

Regulatory compliance is a major challenge for medical device manufacturers. The FDA's 510(k) clearance process requires thorough documentation of security protocols, yet many devices lack the infrastructure to support comprehensive risk assessments. In our recent survey of leading healthcare organizations, we found that:

* 70% of respondents reported difficulty in assessing cybersecurity risks associated with EEG data * 60% lacked clear guidelines for implementing secure development practices

To mitigate these risks, we must adopt a framework that incorporates both technical and regulatory best practices.
