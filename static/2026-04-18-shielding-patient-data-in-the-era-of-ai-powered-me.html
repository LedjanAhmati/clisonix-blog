The Problem: Real Challenges in Data Privacy

While AI-powered medical devices have revolutionized healthcare by enabling real-time monitoring, predictive analysis, and personalized treatment plans, they also introduce new vulnerabilities. Consider the following:

* Distributed nodes and interconnected systems create a complex landscape for security and compliance. * Advanced analytics and machine learning algorithms often rely on vast amounts of sensitive data, increasing the attack surface. * The sheer volume and velocity of patient data necessitate sophisticated data processing and storage solutions.
