The Problem

To address these issues, Clisonix has developed innovative technologies that merge AI with healthcare expertise:

CRDT Merge Layer Medical innovation — Photo: Unsplash

This patented solution enables real-time collaboration among clinicians. By utilizing a Conflict-Free Replicated Data Type (CRDT), the system ensures seamless integration of edits from various users.

Post-Quantum Encryption

Futuristic medical practices require robust security measures. Our Post-Quantum Encryption technology provides a safeguard against future threats, protecting patient data from unauthorized access.

Tide Engine

To guarantee consistency across distributed healthcare nodes, our Tide Engine leverages cutting-edge consensus algorithms. This ensures that all systems are in sync, ensuring accurate and timely decision-making.

Technical Deep Dive

Our EEG and audio analytics solutions rely on a combination of advanced architectures and sophisticated algorithms:

* Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN): For feature extraction from EEG signals. * Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks: To analyze sequential patterns in audio data.

These algorithms are implemented using our custom-built framework, ensuring seamless integration with Clisonix's CRDT Merge Layer and Post-Quantum Encryption technologies. The result is a comprehensive platform for accurate diagnosis and effective treatment planning.
