The Problem: Real Challenges in Audio Processing

Audio analytics have long been touted as the holy grail of healthcare insights. However, extracting actionable information from these recordings is often hindered by technical challenges:

1. Signal quality: Patient recordings are frequently plagued by noise, artifacts, and equipment malfunctions, making it difficult to discern meaningful patterns. 2. Variability: Human speech patterns, breathing rates, and other physiological metrics exhibit inherent variability, complicating the analysis of audio data. 3. Scalability: As healthcare providers seek to leverage AI for predictive analytics, the sheer volume of patient recordings poses significant scalability challenges.

Machine Learning: We employ deep learning algorithms to identify patterns and anomalies in patient data, enabling predictive modeling and real-time alerts. 3. Data Integration: Clisonix's Tide Engine seamlessly integrates with our Signal Fabric platform, allowing for unified processing of EEG, audio, and biosensor streams.
