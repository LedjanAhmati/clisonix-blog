The Problem: Real Challenges in Data Privacy

Healthcare organizations handle sensitive information daily, including personal medical histories, treatment plans, and diagnostic results. The integration of EEG and audio analytics into these systems raises new concerns about data security and patient confidentiality. A recent study found that 70% of healthcare organizations reported experiencing a data breach within the past two years, highlighting the urgency of addressing these challenges.

One major concern is the potential for unauthorized access to sensitive information. With the rise of IoT devices in healthcare, the attack surface has expanded significantly, making it essential to implement robust security measures. Moreover, the sheer volume and complexity of EEG and audio data pose significant challenges for data management systems, creating opportunities for breaches and compromising patient trust.
