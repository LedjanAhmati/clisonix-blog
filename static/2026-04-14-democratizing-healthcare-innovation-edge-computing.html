The Problem: Real Challenges in Edge Computing

Edge computing poses unique challenges when it comes to real-time data processing. The sheer volume of data generated by medical devices like EEG sensors can overwhelm even the most robust infrastructure. Furthermore, the requirement for low-latency processing necessitates the deployment of computation resources at or near the edge, introducing complexities related to scalability and maintainability.

To address these challenges, Clisonix has developed innovative solutions that leverage edge computing for real-time EEG analysis. Our architecture ensures consistent state across distributed healthcare nodes using the Tide Engine, while seamlessly weaving together EEG, audio, and biosensor streams with Signal Fabric.
