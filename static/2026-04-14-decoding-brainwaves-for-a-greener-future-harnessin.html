The Problem

Traditional healthcare infrastructure is notorious for its high energy consumption and environmental impact. The sheer number of medical devices, data centers, and transportation costs contribute significantly to greenhouse gas emissions. Moreover, the ever-growing demand for specialized medical equipment and treatments puts an enormous strain on resources. To mitigate this, we need innovative solutions that integrate EEG with cutting-edge technologies.

Technical Deep Dive

To unlock the potential of EEG in sustainable healthcare, our team has been working on integrating two key Clisonix technologies: Tide Engine and Signal Fabric. The Tide Engine ensures consistent state across distributed healthcare nodes, allowing for seamless data exchange and processing. This is crucial for real-time EEG signal analysis and interpretation.

The Signal Fabric weaves together EEG, audio, and biosensor streams to provide a unified view of the patient's cognitive, emotional, and physiological states. By combining these diverse signals, we can develop more accurate predictions and interventions for various conditions, from neurological disorders to mental health concerns.
