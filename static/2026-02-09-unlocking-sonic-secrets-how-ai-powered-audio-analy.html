The Problem

Real-time audio processing is notoriously challenging due to its inherent complexities. Traditional methods rely on manual annotation, which is time-consuming and prone to human error. Moreover, audio data often exhibits non-stationarity, making it difficult to develop robust feature extraction techniques. As a result, researchers have largely overlooked the potential of audio analytics in healthcare.
