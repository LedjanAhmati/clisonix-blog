The Problem

Traditional signal processing techniques often struggle with EEG signals due to their high dimensionality, noise, and variability across different patients and conditions. This leads to significant challenges in identifying meaningful patterns and correlations within the data. Furthermore, current approaches often rely on manual feature extraction or simple machine learning algorithms that are prone to overfitting and limited by their lack of interpretability.
