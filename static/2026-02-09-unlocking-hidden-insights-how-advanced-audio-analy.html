The Problem: Real Challenges in Audio Processing

Audio processing in healthcare is a complex task due to several reasons:

* Data noise: Audio signals often contain noise, artifacts, and other unwanted features that can mask underlying patterns. * Signal variability: Patients exhibit unique physiological responses to various stimuli, leading to signal variability across different data streams. * Limited domain knowledge: Healthcare professionals may not have the necessary expertise in audio processing and analytics.

To address these challenges, we need advanced audio analytics techniques that can handle complex signals, detect subtle patterns, and provide actionable insights for clinicians.
