The Problem: Real Challenges in Healthcare Tech

Current BCI systems often face significant hurdles:

1. Signal Noise: EEG signals are inherently noisy and sensitive to environmental factors, making it challenging to accurately decode brain activity. 2. Low Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR): The SNR is critical for reliable signal processing; however, existing methods struggle to achieve optimal performance. 3. Limited Scalability: Most BCIs are designed for specific applications or use cases, hindering their adaptability and broader clinical adoption.
