The Problem: Challenges in Audio Processing

Audio processing is a complex task, especially when it comes to healthcare applications. Traditional methods often fall short due to the sheer volume and variability of audio data. Here are some of the challenges:

1. Noise reduction: Removing background noise from recordings without compromising valuable information. 2. Signal extraction: Isolating specific signals or patterns within audio streams. 3. Analysis scalability: Processing large datasets in real-time while maintaining accuracy.
