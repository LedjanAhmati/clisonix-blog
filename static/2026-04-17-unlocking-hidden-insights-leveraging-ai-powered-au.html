The Problem

Current clinical decision support systems (CDSS) rely on manual data entry, often leading to errors, inaccuracies, and delayed diagnoses. Moreover, existing solutions fail to adequately address the nuances of audio data – such as speech patterns, acoustic characteristics, and physiological signals. This is where AI-powered audio analytics can bridge the gap, providing actionable insights that inform clinical decisions.
