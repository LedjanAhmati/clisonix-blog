The Problem: Real Challenges in Audio Processing

Audio signals are a rich source of information for diagnosing and monitoring various health conditions. However, extracting meaningful insights from these signals poses significant challenges. Traditional signal processing techniques often fall short due to limitations such as noise sensitivity, signal fragmentation, and the sheer volume of data generated.

To overcome these hurdles, we need to rethink our approach to audio analytics. This is where AI comes into play – enabling us to develop sophisticated algorithms that can identify patterns in complex soundscapes and provide actionable insights.
