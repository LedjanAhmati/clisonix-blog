Technical Deep Dive

At Clisonix, we have integrated our Tide Engine and Signal Fabric technologies to create a seamless EEG-mediated audio analytics platform. The Tide Engine ensures consistent state across distributed healthcare nodes, enabling real-time data processing and analysis. Meanwhile, the Signal Fabric weaves together EEG, audio, and biosensor streams to provide comprehensive insights into patient behavior and physiological responses.

Our algorithmic framework, built on top of Labor Intelligence Engine (LIAM), utilizes matrix algebra to compute patterns in labor metrics, such as productivity, efficiency, and quality. This enables healthcare professionals to make data-driven decisions about staffing, resource allocation, and treatment planning.
