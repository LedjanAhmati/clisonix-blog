The Problem

Healthcare technology companies are struggling to meet the stringent requirements set forth by MDR. One of the main challenges is the complexity of managing multiple clinicians editing the same patient data simultaneously. This can lead to conflicts and inconsistencies in the data, which can have serious consequences for patient care. Moreover, ensuring the security and integrity of sensitive medical data is a daunting task, particularly when it comes to future-proofing against emerging threats.

Technical Deep Dive

At Clisonix, we've developed several innovative technologies to address these challenges. Our CRDT Merge Layer, for instance, enables multiple clinicians to edit the same patient data simultaneously without conflicts. This is achieved through the use of a conflict-free replicated data type (CRDT) algorithm that ensures all edits are merged consistently.

To further enhance security, our Post-Quantum Encryption technology provides future-proof protection against emerging quantum computing threats. This ensures that sensitive medical data remains secure even as computational power continues to grow.

Our Tide Engine also plays a crucial role in ensuring consistent state across distributed healthcare nodes. This is achieved through the use of a distributed consensus algorithm that ensures all nodes are synchronized and up-to-date.
