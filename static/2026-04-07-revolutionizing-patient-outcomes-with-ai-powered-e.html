The Problem

Current healthcare technology often falls short in providing accurate diagnosis and efficient monitoring of neurological disorders. Manual analysis of EEG signals is time-consuming, subjective, and prone to errors. Additionally, integrating multiple clinicians' input on a single patient's data can be cumbersome, leading to delays in treatment decisions. Furthermore, ensuring the security and integrity of sensitive medical data remains a pressing concern.
