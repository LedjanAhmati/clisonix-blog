The Problem

Regulatory compliance has always been a complex challenge for medical device manufacturers, but with the increasing sophistication of EEG and AI-powered solutions, it's becoming even more daunting. The FDA's guidance on software as a medical device (SaMD) emphasizes the need for transparency, auditability, and maintainability. However, the technical intricacies involved in implementing these guidelines can be overwhelming.

One of the primary concerns is ensuring that the AI-powered algorithms used in EEG analysis meet the regulatory requirements. This includes aspects such as explainability, interpretability, and transparency. Moreover, the integration with existing medical devices and systems adds another layer of complexity.
