The Problem: Real Challenges in Sustainable Tech

Moreover, the rapid adoption of AI and machine learning (ML) models has led to increased computing demands, further exacerbating energy consumption.

To mitigate these challenges, it's essential to focus on energy-efficient model architectures that can optimize resource utilization while maintaining or even improving performance.
