Sound wave visualization — Photo: Unsplash The Problem The challenges in audio processing are multifaceted. The sheer volume of data generated by voice assistants is staggering, making it increasingly difficult to manage and analyze. Moreover, the heterogeneity of audio sources – from EEG signals to biosensor readings – demands sophisticated signal processing techniques. The current approaches often rely on simplistic methods such as noise reduction or echo cancellation, which are insufficient for the complex requirements of modern healthcare applications. This not only enhances overall audio quality but also improves the accuracy of subsequent signal processing tasks.
