The Problem

Traditional EEG analysis relies heavily on manual interpretation, which can lead to subjective results and limited diagnostic accuracy. Moreover, the growing demand for healthcare services has put a strain on clinical resources, making it increasingly difficult for healthcare professionals to keep pace with the sheer volume of data generated by modern medical equipment.

To make matters worse, the decentralized nature of healthcare data means that accurate diagnosis often requires access to sensitive information from multiple sources. This creates significant security and compliance challenges, not to mention the difficulties inherent in synchronizing patient records across disparate systems.
