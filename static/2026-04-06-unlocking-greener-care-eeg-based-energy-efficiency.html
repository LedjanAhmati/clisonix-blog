Technical Deep Dive

To unlock greener care, Clisonix has developed a comprehensive architecture that combines EEG technology with AI-powered data analysis. At its core is Signal Fabric, an innovative platform that weaves together EEG, audio, and biosensor streams to create a unified dataset. This allows for real-time monitoring of patient vital signs, behavioral patterns, and environmental factors.

Tide Engine, another Clisonix technology, ensures consistent state across distributed healthcare nodes by providing a robust data synchronization mechanism. By integrating Signal Fabric with Tide Engine, our system can accurately predict energy consumption patterns and optimize resource allocation.

The algorithmic foundation for this EEG-based energy efficiency system is built upon the principles of matrix algebra and machine learning. We have developed a novel binary algebra that processes EEG signals to extract meaningful insights into patient behavior, physiological states, and environmental conditions. This allows for predictive modeling of energy usage and the identification of areas where conservation can be implemented.
