Technical Deep Dive: Architecture, Algorithms, Implementation

At the heart of our RPM solution lies our CRDT (Conflict-free Replicated Data Type) Merge Layer. This technology enables multiple clinicians to edit patient data simultaneously without conflicts or errors. By utilizing a CRDT-based approach, we ensure that all stakeholders have access to up-to-date information, reducing miscommunication and errors.

Our Post-Quantum Encryption capabilities provide future-proof security for sensitive medical data. This means that even as quantum computing advances, our encryption methods will remain secure, protecting patient confidentiality and integrity.

Tide Engine is another critical component of our RPM solution. It ensures consistent state across distributed healthcare nodes, guaranteeing that all stakeholders have access to the same information in real-time.
