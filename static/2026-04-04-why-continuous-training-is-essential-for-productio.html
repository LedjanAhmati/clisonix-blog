The Problem: Real Challenges in AI ML Systems

Continuous training is crucial because AI models are inherently data-dependent and prone to forgetting or drifting away from their intended behavior over time. This phenomenon is particularly pronounced in healthcare, where the underlying distributions of patient data and clinical practices evolve constantly.

Moreover, traditional machine learning approaches often rely on batch processing and periodic updates, which can be inflexible and inadequate for real-time applications like AI-assisted diagnosis. These limitations can lead to decreased model accuracy, increased latency, and compromised decision-making capabilities.
