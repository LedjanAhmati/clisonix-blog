The Problem: Real Challenges in Edge Computing

Edge computing promises to revolutionize healthcare by reducing latency, increasing data processing capabilities, and improving real-time decision-making. However, several challenges hinder its adoption:

1. Scalability: Healthcare data is vast and diverse, requiring edge devices to handle multiple streams of data from various sources. 2. Complexity: Edge devices need to integrate with existing infrastructure, including IoT sensors, medical imaging equipment, and clinical systems. 3. Security: Edge devices are exposed to the internet, making them vulnerable to cyber threats.

To overcome these challenges, Clisonix has developed a comprehensive edge AI architecture that leverages our flagship technologies: Tide Engine and Signal Fabric.

Technical Deep Dive: Architecture, Algorithms, Implementation

1. Data Ingestion: Our Signal Fabric technology collects data from various sources, including EEG, audio, and biosensor streams. 2.
