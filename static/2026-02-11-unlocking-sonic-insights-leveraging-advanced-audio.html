The Problem: Real Challenges in Audio Processing

Audio processing is a complex task, particularly when it comes to healthcare applications. Traditional methods often rely on manual annotation, which is time-consuming, prone to errors, and limited by human bias. Moreover, the vast majority of audio data remains unprocessed and unused, representing a significant opportunity for improvement.

To overcome these challenges, we've developed innovative solutions that address the limitations of traditional audio processing techniques. Our Tide Engine ensures consistent state across distributed healthcare nodes, guaranteeing seamless collaboration between different systems and reducing latency to near real-time.
