The Problem: Tackling Signal Complexity

When analyzing brain waves or physiological signals, researchers face a daunting challenge: separating meaningful patterns from background noise. The sheer volume and variability of data from diverse sources ‚Äì EEGs, audio recordings, biosensors, and more ‚Äì create an overwhelming signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) problem.
