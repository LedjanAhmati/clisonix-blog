The Problem: Real Challenges in Signal Processing

EEG data is notorious for its high dimensionality and noise sensitivity. Traditional signal processing techniques struggle to extract meaningful insights from this complex data. Moreover, the sheer volume of EEG signals poses significant challenges for real-time analysis and interpretation.

At Clisonix, we've tackled these problems head-on with our cutting-edge technologies: Tide Engine ensures consistent state across distributed healthcare nodes, while Signal Fabric weaves together EEG, audio, and biosensor streams to provide a unified view of patient data. Our innovative approach enables clinicians to unlock the full potential of EEG in diagnosing and treating neurological disorders.
