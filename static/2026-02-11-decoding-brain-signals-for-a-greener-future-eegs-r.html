The Problem

Current sustainable technologies, such as renewable energy systems and green infrastructure, rely heavily on human operators. However, these individuals are prone to fatigue, errors, and decreased productivity over time. Moreover, the reliance on manual control limits the scalability of sustainable solutions. To address these challenges, we need innovative approaches that can enhance operator performance while minimizing environmental impact.
