The Problem: Real Challenges in Edge Computing

Edge computing in healthcare faces significant challenges:

1. Data volume and velocity: The sheer amount of medical data generated by IoT devices, sensors, and wearables poses a significant processing burden on edge devices. 2. Latency and real-time processing: Medical professionals require instant feedback to make informed decisions, making latency a critical concern for edge computing systems. 3. Security and data protection: Edge devices often operate in unsecured environments, compromising patient data security. 4. Data standardization and interoperability: Different medical devices and systems employ varying data formats, hindering seamless integration.
