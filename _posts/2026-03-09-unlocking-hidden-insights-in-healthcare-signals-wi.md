**The Problem: Challenges in Signal Processing**

Signal processing is a crucial component of healthcare data analysis, enabling clinicians to identify patterns and anomalies in patient data. However, working with EEG and audio signals presents several challenges:

1. **Noise and Interference**: EEG signals are prone to electrical noise from various sources, including the environment and instrumentation.
2. **High-Dimensional Data**: Multimodal datasets (e.g., EEG + audio) generate vast amounts of high-dimensional data, making it difficult to extract meaningful insights.
3. **Variability in Signal Properties**: Signals exhibit varying properties such as amplitude, frequency, and time domain characteristics.
