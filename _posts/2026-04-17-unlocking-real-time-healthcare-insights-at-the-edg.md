**The Problem**

Edge computing has shown tremendous promise in enabling real-time processing, reducing latency, and minimizing network traffic. However, most existing solutions rely heavily on cloud-based infrastructure, which introduces several challenges:

*   **Latency**: Cloud-based solutions require data to be transmitted to the cloud for processing, introducing significant latency that can compromise real-time analytics.
*   **Bandwidth**: The constant stream of medical data from various sources increases bandwidth requirements, making it difficult to maintain a stable and secure network connection.
*   **Data Sovereignty**: Healthcare institutions are increasingly concerned about maintaining control over sensitive patient data. Cloud-based solutions often require data to be transmitted outside the institution's premises.
