**The Problem**

Traditional ML pipelines often break down when moving from development to deployment. The lack of robustness in model training, data quality issues, and inadequate infrastructure lead to suboptimal performance, latency, and a host of other problems. To make matters worse, the increasing complexity of modern AI systems has created new challenges:

1. **Scalability**: As models grow in size and complexity, deployment becomes increasingly difficult.
2. **Interoperability**: Integration with existing infrastructure and data sources is often plagued by compatibility issues.
3. **Explainability**: Lacking insight into model decisions makes it hard to debug and optimize.

These challenges are further exacerbated by the growing need for real-time inference, edge-to-cloud deployment, and seamless integration with diverse data sources.
