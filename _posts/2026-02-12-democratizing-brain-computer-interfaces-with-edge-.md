**The Problem: Challenges in Edge Computing**

Edge computing has emerged as a crucial enabler for real-time applications that require low-latency processing and data analysis. However, several challenges persist in the realm of edge computing:

1.  **Scalability**: As the number of devices connected to the network increases, managing and maintaining these devices becomes a significant challenge.
2.  **Resource Constraints**: Edge devices often have limited computational resources, storage capacity, and power supply, which can hinder their ability to process complex tasks efficiently.
3.  **Data Security**: The transmission of sensitive medical data across networks raises concerns regarding data privacy and security.
