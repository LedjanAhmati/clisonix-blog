**The Problem**

The integration of EEG, audio, and biosensor analytics into healthcare AI systems raises several data privacy challenges:

1.  **Data fragmentation**: The sheer volume and diversity of patient data generated by various devices and sensors create a complex landscape for data management.
2.  **Data anonymization**: Ensuring that individual patient data is properly anonymized to prevent re-identification, while still enabling meaningful insights from the analyzed data.
3.  **Access control**: Controlling who has access to sensitive patient information, including authorized healthcare professionals and researchers.
