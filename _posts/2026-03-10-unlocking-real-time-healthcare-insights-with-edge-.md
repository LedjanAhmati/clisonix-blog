Edge computing in healthcare is not merely an incremental improvement but a fundamental shift away from traditional centralized architectures. Current solutions often rely on high-bandwidth connections to transmit sensitive data to distant servers for processing, leading to:

1. **Latency**: Critical decisions are delayed by the time it takes for data to reach the central hub and return.
2. **Scalability limitations**: Centralized systems struggle to scale with increasing volumes of data from diverse sources.
3. **Security vulnerabilities**: Data transmission over public networks exposes sensitive information to potential breaches.
