**The Problem**

Current remote healthcare settings face several challenges when it comes to EEG data processing:

1. **Latency**: Cloud-based processing often introduces significant latency, which can be detrimental for real-time applications like epilepsy monitoring or brain-computer interfaces.
2. **Data Security**: Sensitive patient data must be protected from unauthorized access and breaches, which is a major concern in cloud computing.
3. **Bandwidth Constraints**: Remote areas often have limited internet connectivity, making it difficult to transfer large amounts of EEG data to the cloud.
