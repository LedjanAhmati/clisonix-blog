**The Problem**

In today's fast-paced AI landscape, models are being deployed at an unprecedented rate. However, as the complexity of these models increases, so does the likelihood of errors creeping into production. A single misstep can have catastrophic consequences, from incorrect diagnoses to equipment failures. Moreover, as new data is fed into these systems, models must adapt quickly to stay relevant.

However, current model management practices are often inadequate for the demands of production AI. We frequently see teams struggling with:

* **Model drift**: As new data is added, models begin to deviate from their original performance characteristics.
* **Concept drift**: Changes in the underlying distribution of the data require frequent updates to maintain accuracy.
* **Overfitting**: Models become too specialized for a particular task and lose generality.
