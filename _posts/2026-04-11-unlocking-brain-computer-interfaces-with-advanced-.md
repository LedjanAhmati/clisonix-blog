**The Problem: Real Challenges in Signal Processing**

Current BCI systems face numerous challenges related to signal noise, artifacts, and variability. For instance:

* **Electroencephalography (EEG) noise**: EEG signals are prone to interference from various sources, including electromyographic activity, electrode movement, and power line noise.
* **Signal drift**: EEG signals can gradually change over time due to factors like electrode degradation or changes in the subject's state.
* **Variability**: EEG signals exhibit significant inter-subject variability, making it difficult to develop algorithms that generalize across different individuals.
