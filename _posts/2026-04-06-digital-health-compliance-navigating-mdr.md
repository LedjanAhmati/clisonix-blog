**The Problem: Challenges in Healthcare Tech**

Healthcare technology companies face a multitude of compliance hurdles, from data protection to software validation. The European Union's Medical Device Regulation (MDR) specifically requires medical device manufacturers to ensure the safety and performance of their products throughout their lifecycle. This includes aspects such as:

* **Software as a Medical Device (SaMD)**: Ensuring that software applications are designed and developed in accordance with MDR requirements.
* **Clinical Evaluation**: Conducting thorough clinical evaluations to demonstrate the safety and efficacy of medical devices.
* **Post-market Surveillance**: Continuously monitoring and evaluating the performance of medical devices after they have been placed on the market.

These challenges can lead to regulatory non-compliance, financial penalties, and damage to a company's reputation. To address these concerns, we'll examine how Clisonix's technologies can help healthcare tech companies meet MDR requirements.
