**The Problem: Real Challenges in Edge Computing**

Edge computing is a distributed system architecture that enables data processing to occur at or near the source of the data, reducing latency and improving overall performance. However, when it comes to real-time EEG signal processing, edge computing faces several challenges:

1. **Data Volume and Velocity**: EEG signals generate vast amounts of high-frequency data, which must be processed in real-time to provide meaningful insights.
2. **Network Latency**: Edge nodes often rely on wireless connectivity, introducing latency that can compromise the accuracy of AI models.
3. **Computational Complexity**: Advanced AI algorithms for signal processing require significant computational resources, making them unsuitable for edge devices.

To overcome these challenges, Clisonix has developed innovative solutions that harness the power of edge computing.
