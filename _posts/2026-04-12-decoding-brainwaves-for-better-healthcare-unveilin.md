**The Problem**

Current EEG signal processing methods rely heavily on traditional frequency-domain analysis, which has limitations in capturing the complex dynamics of brain activity. The sheer volume of data generated by modern EEG devices makes it difficult to detect subtle patterns indicative of neurological conditions. Moreover, the variability in electrode placement and individual differences in brain anatomy can significantly impact signal quality.

To overcome these challenges, we need a more sophisticated approach that can accurately capture and analyze EEG signals. Clisonix's Signal Fabric technology weaves together EEG, audio, and biosensor streams to create a unified framework for data fusion and processing.
