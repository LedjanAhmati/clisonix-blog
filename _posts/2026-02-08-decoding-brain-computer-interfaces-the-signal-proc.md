**The Problem: Real Challenges in Signal Processing**

Signal processing is a critical component of BCI development, as it enables the extraction of relevant information from noisy neural signals. However, several challenges hinder the accurate decoding of brain activity:

1. **Noisy Signals**: EEG signals are prone to interference from external sources, such as muscle activity, eye movements, and electromagnetic radiation.
2. **Non-Stationarity**: Brain activity is dynamic, and signals can change over time due to various factors like attention or emotions.
3. **Dimensionality**: High-dimensional neural data pose challenges for analysis and interpretation.
