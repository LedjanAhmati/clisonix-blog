**The Problem**

Large-scale AI deployments in healthcare, finance, and other industries are plagued by issues such as:

1. **Data silos**: Fragmented data landscapes hinder collaboration and make it difficult to integrate disparate systems.
2. **Model drift**: As data distributions change over time, models become inaccurate, leading to suboptimal decision-making.
3. **Scalability challenges**: Legacy architectures struggle to handle increasing compute demands, resulting in decreased performance and user experience.

To address these concerns, organizations need a comprehensive approach to AI governance that encompasses data management, model monitoring, and infrastructure optimization.
