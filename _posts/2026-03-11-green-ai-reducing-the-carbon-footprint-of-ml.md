**The Problem**

The problem lies in the very nature of machine learning itself. Traditional ML models rely on vast amounts of computational resources, often running on high-power servers that guzzle electricity and generate massive carbon emissions. Moreover, the ever-growing demand for data storage, processing, and transmission contributes to e-waste and resource depletion. At Clisonix, we've seen firsthand how these challenges can be addressed through innovative technologies like our Tide Engine and Signal Fabric.
