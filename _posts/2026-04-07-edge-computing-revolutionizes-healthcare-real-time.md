**The Problem**

Traditional EEG analysis relies on centralized data processing, which introduces latency and hinders timely decision-making. Data must be transmitted to a remote server for analysis, only to be returned hours or even days later. This approach is not only inefficient but also compromises the accuracy of the results due to data degradation during transmission.

Furthermore, existing edge computing solutions often lack the necessary scalability and adaptability to handle the demands of medical applications. They may require significant infrastructure investments, which can be cost-prohibitive for healthcare organizations.
