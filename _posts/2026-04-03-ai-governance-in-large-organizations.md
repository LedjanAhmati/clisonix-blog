**The Problem**

Large organizations face unique challenges when it comes to AI governance. The sheer scale and complexity of their systems make it difficult to maintain transparency, accountability, and explainability. Here are just a few examples:

1. **Data silos**: As data is scattered across various departments and systems, ensuring that all stakeholders have access to accurate and up-to-date information becomes a significant challenge.
2. **Model drift**: Changes in the underlying data distribution or model architecture can cause AI models to degrade over time, compromising their accuracy and reliability.
3. **Regulatory compliance**: Keeping pace with evolving regulations and standards is a daunting task for large organizations, particularly when it comes to sensitive domains like healthcare.
