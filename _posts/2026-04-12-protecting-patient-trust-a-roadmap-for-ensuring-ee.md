**The Problem**

EEG and medical device data are particularly vulnerable to cyber threats due to their sensitive nature and the vast amounts of personal health information (PHI) they contain. With the exponential growth of connected devices in healthcare, vulnerabilities such as inadequate access controls, weak authentication mechanisms, and outdated software can be exploited by malicious actors.

Moreover, the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) into these systems has introduced new challenges. As AI algorithms process and analyze vast amounts of medical data, there is an inherent risk that sensitive information may be compromised or used inappropriately.
