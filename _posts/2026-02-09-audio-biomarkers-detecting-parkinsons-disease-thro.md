**The Problem: Challenges in Audio Processing**

Audio processing is a complex field, requiring expertise in signal processing, machine learning, and data analysis. However, when it comes to detecting diseases like Parkinson's, there are several challenges that must be addressed:

* **Noise reduction**: Speech signals are often contaminated with background noise, making it difficult to extract meaningful patterns.
* **Variability**: Individuals with Parkinson's disease may exhibit varying levels of speech difficulties, making it challenging to develop a robust detection system.
* **Interpretation**: Audio biomarkers must be interpreted in the context of the individual's overall health profile.
