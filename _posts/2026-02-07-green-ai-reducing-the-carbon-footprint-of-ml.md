**The Problem: Unsustainable Tech**

The current state of AI development often prioritizes computational power over environmental concerns. This can be attributed to several factors:

1.  **Training datasets**: Large-scale ML models require vast amounts of data, which are frequently generated through resource-intensive processes.

3.  **Hardware requirements**: The increasing need for specialized hardware, such as Graphics Processing Units (GPUs) and Tensor Processing Units (TPUs), contributes to the environmental footprint of AI.
