**The Problem**

One of the most significant challenges facing developers working with sensitive patient data is ensuring compliance with GDPR's strict guidelines. The regulation mandates that organizations must demonstrate a clear understanding of their data processing activities, including how personal data is collected, stored, and used for machine learning.

Clisonix has faced this challenge head-on by developing innovative solutions for building GDPR-compliant machine learning pipelines. Our approach to compliant machine learning begins with the creation of a data processing inventory (DPI).
