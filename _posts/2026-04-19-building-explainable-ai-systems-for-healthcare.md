**The Problem**

Current AI/ML systems face significant challenges when it comes to explainability. Many models rely on complex algorithms and proprietary techniques that are difficult to understand, even for experts in the field. This lack of transparency can lead to several issues:

1. **Trust erosion**: Healthcare professionals may question the validity of AI-driven decisions if they don't understand how those decisions were made.
2. **Regulatory compliance**: Hospitals and medical institutions must comply with regulations like HIPAA and GDPR, which require transparent data handling practices.
3. **Liability concerns**: In cases where AI-driven decisions lead to adverse outcomes, healthcare providers may be held liable.
