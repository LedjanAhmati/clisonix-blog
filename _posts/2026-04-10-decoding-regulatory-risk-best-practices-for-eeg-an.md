**The Problem: Real Challenges in Regulatory Compliance**

Regulatory compliance is a critical aspect of developing and deploying AI-powered medical devices, particularly those utilizing electroencephalography (EEG) signals. The FDA's guidance emphasizes the importance of ensuring device safety and effectiveness, as well as maintaining accurate and reliable data processing. However, EEG-based medical devices introduce unique challenges:

1. **Signal complexity**: EEG signals are inherently noisy and complex, making it difficult to ensure consistent and accurate signal processing.
2. **Data integration**: Combining EEG with other biosensor streams, such as audio or ECG, introduces additional complexities in terms of data fusion and synchronization.
3. **Algorithms and architecture**: Developing AI-powered devices requires sophisticated algorithms that meet regulatory requirements for transparency and explainability.
