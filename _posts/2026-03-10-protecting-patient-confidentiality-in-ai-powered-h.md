**The Problem**

Patient confidentiality is a fundamental principle in healthcare, governed by regulations such as HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) in the United States. However, the rise of AI-powered systems introduces new risks to confidentiality:

1. **Data sharing**: AI algorithms often require large amounts of data to function effectively, which can lead to unauthorized access or exposure of sensitive patient information.
2. **Data storage**: The increasing volume of health-related data generated by AI-powered systems poses significant storage and security challenges.
3. **Data transfer**: As healthcare providers increasingly rely on cloud-based services, the risk of data breaches and unauthorized access grows.
