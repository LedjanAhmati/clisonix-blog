**The Problem: Challenges in Current AI/ML Systems**

Traditional AI/ML systems rely on complex algorithms and opaque models that make it difficult to understand how decisions are made. This lack of transparency raises significant concerns about accountability, reliability, and trustworthiness. In healthcare, where lives depend on accurate diagnoses and treatments, this issue is particularly critical.

1. **Black Box Models**: Current AI/ML systems often employ deep neural networks with millions of parameters, making it nearly impossible to interpret their internal workings.
2. **Lack of Transparency**: The lack of explainability hinders clinicians' ability to trust AI-driven recommendations, leading to hesitancy in adopting these systems.
3. **Interpretability Gaps**: The absence of clear explanations for model decisions creates a knowledge gap between data scientists and clinicians, hindering collaboration and improvement.
