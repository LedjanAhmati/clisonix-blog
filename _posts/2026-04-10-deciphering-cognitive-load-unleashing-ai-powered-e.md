**The Problem**

Cognitive load, a concept born from psychology, refers to the amount of mental effort required to complete a task or achieve a specific goal. In enterprise healthcare settings, it's not uncommon for staff members to juggle multiple tasks simultaneously, including clinical decision-making, administrative duties, and data management. This perpetual state of multitasking can lead to decreased productivity, reduced accuracy, and an increased risk of burnout.

Traditional solutions have attempted to address cognitive load through manual process optimization or the implementation of basic automation tools. However, these approaches often fall short in addressing the complex, adaptive nature of healthcare workflows. The dynamic interplay between patients, staff, equipment, and administrative systems necessitates a more sophisticated approach – one that leverages artificial intelligence (AI) to dynamically adapt and optimize cognitive load.
