As we continue to push the boundaries of AI-powered healthcare solutions, it's essential to acknowledge the environmental cost associated with large language models (LLMs). At Clisonix, we're committed to developing sustainable technologies that minimize the ecological footprint of our Labor Intelligence & Analytics Machine (LIAM) systems.

**The Problem**

Large language models have revolutionized the field of natural language processing, enabling applications like text classification, sentiment analysis, and language translation. However, their massive size and complexity come with a significant environmental cost:

1. **Energy Consumption**: Training and running LLMs require vast amounts of energy, contributing to greenhouse gas emissions and exacerbating climate change.
2. **Data Storage**: The storage needs for these models are substantial, requiring large-scale data centers that consume significant amounts of power and produce e-waste.
3. **Resource Intensity**: The computational resources required for training and running LLMs lead to increased energy consumption and strain on the grid.
