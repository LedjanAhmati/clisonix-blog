**The Problem**
AI and ML systems are inherently complex, relying on intricate networks of interconnected components. These complexities introduce vulnerabilities that can be exploited by malicious actors. Traditional security measures often focus on individual components, rather than the system as a whole. As a result, security breaches can occur through even seemingly minor flaws.

To address this challenge, we need to adopt a more holistic approach to AI and ML system design. This is where coherence analysis comes in – a technique that assesses the relationships between model parameters, input data, and output predictions. By analyzing these relationships, developers can identify potential vulnerabilities before they become critical issues.
