Adaptive filtering is an advanced signal processing technique designed to mitigate the effects of noise and interference in real-time audio streams. 2. **Model Training**: A machine learning model is trained on a dataset of known threats and noise patterns to learn optimal filtering parameters.
3. **Adaptive Filtering**: The trained model adjusts the filtering parameters based on changing environmental conditions, ensuring optimal performance.
