**Technical Deep Dive**

ICA is an unsupervised machine learning algorithm that separates mixed signals into individual components. By analyzing the statistical dependencies between audio streams, ICA can identify and extract distinct sources of sound. In the context of security systems, ICA can be applied to:

1.  **Noisy Environments**: ICA can separate noise from relevant audio signals, improving signal-to-noise ratios (SNRs) and reducing false alarms.
2.  **Multiple Sound Sources**: By identifying individual components, ICA can help distinguish between multiple sound sources, such as sirens, alarms, or background chatter.
3.  **Varying Acoustic Properties**: ICA's ability to adapt to changing acoustic environments ensures consistent audio quality across different settings.
