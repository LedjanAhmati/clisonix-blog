**The Problem**

Real-world challenges to securing EEG data are multifaceted:

1.  **Data Fragmentation**: Medical devices often operate independently, making it difficult to centralize and manage EEG data.
2.  **Device Heterogeneity**: Integrating EEG data from various devices and manufacturers can lead to compatibility issues and security vulnerabilities.
3.  **Regulatory Compliance**: Meeting stringent regulatory requirements for data protection, such as GDPR and HIPAA, is a significant concern.
