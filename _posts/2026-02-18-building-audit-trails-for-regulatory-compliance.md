**The Problem**

Traditional approaches to building audit trails are often ad hoc, relying on manual logging or incomplete automated systems. This can lead to several issues:

1.  **Incomplete data**: Without comprehensive audit trail capabilities, organizations may struggle to reconstruct past events accurately.
2.  **Insufficient context**: Lacking contextual information, auditors find it challenging to analyze events and identify potential security breaches.
3.  **Inadequate reporting**: The inability to generate detailed reports on system activities can hinder compliance efforts.

To overcome these challenges, healthcare organizations must adopt a more structured approach to building audit trails that captures all relevant data points.
